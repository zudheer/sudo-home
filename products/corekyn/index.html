CoreKyn

A workout logging and coaching app — training circles, progress tracking, and coach–student programs, backed by a FastAPI service handling auth, billing and social features.

View more details ↗ Get it on Google Play

The idea

Most workout apps are either a bare set-and-rep logger or a bloated all-in-one platform. CoreKyn sits in between: a training log you'll actually keep using, with just enough social structure — training circles and coach–student programs — to keep you accountable, without turning into a social network.

What we built

01

Workout logging & history

Sets, reps, weight, and a full training history synced across devices.

02

Exercise library

A structured, Redis-cached exercise database to build routines from — fast even offline.

03

Training circles & coaching

Connect with training partners, or get assigned to a coach who guides your program.

04

Share via QR / link

Send a workout or profile instantly — no account required for the recipient to view it.

05

Subscriptions

Free tier plus a Pro upgrade, billed through both Google Play Billing and Stripe, with server-side receipt verification.

06

Security & abuse prevention

Phone-verified accounts, Play Integrity checks, and rate-limited auth endpoints behind an nginx layer.

Under the hood

The backend is a FastAPI service (4 uvicorn workers) behind nginx, with PostgreSQL for storage and Redis for caching and OTP handling. A separate cron container runs scheduled jobs — subscription expiry, cleanup — daily via APScheduler. Auth uses Firebase phone verification; payments run through Stripe and Google/Apple in-app purchase verification in parallel, so the same subscription works regardless of which store a user paid through.
