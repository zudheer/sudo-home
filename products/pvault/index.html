PVault

A private diary and finance tracker, encrypted on your device and locked behind your fingerprint or face — nothing leaves your phone unless you choose to back it up.

View more details ↗ Get notified at launch

The idea

Diary apps and finance trackers are usually two separate apps, and both usually ask you to trust a server with fairly personal data. PVault keeps both in one private vault — encrypted locally, unlocked with biometrics, with cloud backup as an explicit opt-in rather than a default.

What we're building

01

Diary & notes

Entries organized into sections, so journal, plans, and private notes each have their own space.

02

Finance tracker

Income and expenses logged alongside the diary — one private app for both.

03

Biometric lock

Fingerprint or face unlock via the device's own OS-level biometric APIs.

04

Local encryption

Entries encrypted at rest on-device — not just hidden behind a lock screen.

05

Recycle bin

Deleted entries can be recovered before they're gone for good.

06

Free & Pro tiers

Free with ads, or PVault Pro for an ad-free experience plus optional Google Drive backup.

Under the hood

PVault ships as two apps from one Flutter codebase — a free, ad-supported build and a Pro build with no ads and cloud backup. Data lives in a local, encrypted SQLite database; biometric unlock is handled entirely by the OS, so the app never touches raw fingerprint or face data. When Pro's cloud backup is enabled, an encrypted copy is written to a folder the app creates in the user's own Google Drive — never to a server we run.
